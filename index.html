Frequently Asked Questions

~Is Foodies Bread really baked fresh each day?

Foodies Bread claims that their bread is baked fresh each day, it's likely that they follow a daily baking schedule to ensure that their bread is as fresh as possible. Many bakeries do follow this practice as it ensures that the bread is at its best quality and flavor. It's always best to ask the bakery directly about their baking practices to confirm if their bread is baked fresh each day.

~Do you bake breads containing animal fats or products?

The use of animal fats or products in bread is a common practice in many bakeries, especially when making certain types of bread such as brioche or challah. Animal fats such as butter or lard can add flavor and richness to bread, while eggs and milk can add moisture and texture. If you have specific dietary restrictions or preferences, it's always best to ask the bakery directly about the ingredients they use in their bread to ensure that it meets your needs. Many bakeries also offer vegan or plant-based options, so it's worth checking if they have any suitable alternatives.
